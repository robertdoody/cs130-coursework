Dachshund

  • Dachshunds are originally from Germany, where they were bred to hunt badgers about 300 years ago

  • Dachshunds are the smallest dogs that are used for hunting

  • The first Olympic mascot was a dachsund named Waldi in 1972

  • It is believed hot dogs were originally known as dachshund sausages since they beared a resemblance to the long body of the dog

  • They are the 10th most popular breed in the US, but they were as high as 5th in 2003
