Welsh Corgi

  • There are two different kinds of Welsh corgis: Pembroke Welsh and Welsh Cardigan. They are considered distinct because they originate from different ancestors

  • The Welsh originally used corgis as herding dogs beginning in the 10th century

  • The name Corgi comes from Welsh, where "cor" means "dwarf" and "gi" means "dog"

  • Queen Elizabeth II has owned more than 30 different corgis

  • Sourthern California holds an corgi meetup called Corgi Beach Day three times a year, where over 1000 corgis attend
