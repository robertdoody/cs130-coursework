Chihuahua

  • They are, on average, the smallest dog in the world. They can weigh between 2-6 pounds, and stand at about 5 inches tall

  • Chihuahua come from an ancient breed of dog, the techichi, which were originally from what is now Mexico

  • In the past, chihuahuas used to be larger, but through crossbreeding with a small hairless dog from Asia, they began to shrink in size

  • Compared to other dog breeds, chihuahuas have the biggest brains relative to their size

  • In 2014, a pack of stray chihuahuas overran and terrorized a small town in Arizona. There were 3 times the calls to Animal Control in that town compared to the surrounding areas
