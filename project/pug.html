Pug

  • It's believed the origin of pugs dates back to China before 400 BCE

  • Emperors of China treated their pugs like royalty, giving them luxeries such as their own palaces and guards

  • A group of pugs is called a grumble

  • Pugs are known for their smushed faces, but these can lead to breathing issues. This is why they tend to snuffle while running around

  • Pugs are commonly know to be lazy. They can sleep for up to 14 hours a day
