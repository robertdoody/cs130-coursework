Beagle

  • The first beagles were only aobut 8 or 9 inches tall. It wasn't until later they were bred into the hunting dogs they are today

  • As of 2014, beagles were the fifth most popular breed in the US

  • Beagle means "loudmouth" in French, and they are known for being extremely vocal dogs

  • Purebred beagles have white-tipped tails, even if it is only a few hairs. This is used to more easily track them in the woods and in tall grass

  • Some popular beagles are Odie from Garfield, Snoopy from Peanuts, and the titular dog from Shiloh
