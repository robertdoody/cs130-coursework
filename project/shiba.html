Shiba Inu

  • Shibas are the #1 companion dog in Japan

  • During WWII, the breed almost went extinct because Japan's focus was not on dog breeding

  • Shiba Inus didn't arrive in the US until 1972

  • Shibas are considered the "drama-queens" of all the breeds because they let out human-like screams when under stress. Some stressors include taking a bath or clipping their nails

  • They were bred to be hunting dogs, hunting specifically for boards, birds, and bears. They have dense undercoats, which helps them stay warm in cold temperatures
