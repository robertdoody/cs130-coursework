Bernese Mountain Dog

  • The name Bernese Mountain Dog refers to the region Bern Canton of Switzerland, where they were originally bred

  • Bernese Mountain Dogs can pull up to 1000 pounds - 10 times their own weight

  • They have a sense of humor! If they do something that makes their owner laugh, they are more likely to repeat that action

  • Bernese Mountain Dogs are particulary affectionate with children, including having the patience to let kids crawl over them

  • The breed was the 32nd most popular in the US in 2013, and was the 11th in Germany in 2014
