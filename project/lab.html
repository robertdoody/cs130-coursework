Labrador Retriever

  • As of 2015, Labradors are the most popular breed in the US

  • They can reach a running speed of 12 mph in only 3 seconds

  • Labs are the most commonly used dog as guide dogs

  • Labradors have been trained to sniff out early stages of cancer. They do this by smelling a patient's breath, blood, and excrement

  • There are 2 genes that account for their fur color, so you can easily get black, yellow, and chocolate puppies in one litter
