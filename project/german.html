German Shepard

  • German Shepards are considered the 3rd most intelligent dog breed in the world

  • In the 80's, there was a play titled Going to the Dogs that starred 6 German Shepards that were all sent to drama lessons prior to learn how to act

  • The first recorded instance of a seeing eye dog was a German Shepard named Buddy in the 1920's

  • German Shepards were originally sheparding dogs, but around the turn of the Industrial Revolution, they became more fitted to work with police departments. service workers, and army officers

  • They were the 2nd most popular breed in the US in 2014
