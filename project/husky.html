Husky

  • Huskies have incredibly high endurance, resistance to the cold, and the ability to survive on little food. Because of this, they are the favored breed to be used as sled dogs

  • A distinct feature of Huskies is their piercing blue eyes. Some other breeds have this feature, but it is most common among Huskies

  • They love to run and explore, which means you have to keep a close eye on them. They are known to easily be able to dig under fences and slip out of their leashes

  • A howl from a Husky can be heard up to 10 miles away

  • Huskies are an extremely friendly breed, including to strangers. Because of this, they don't tend to make great guard dogs
