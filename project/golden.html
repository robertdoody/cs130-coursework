Golden Retriever

  • It was believed Golden Retrievers originated from Russia, but they actually descended from dogs from Scotland

  • Goldens tend to mature at a slower rate than other breeds, which is why they keep their "puppy-like" behavior into adulthood

  • They are most commonly employed as therapy dogs because of their empathy and love towards humans

  • Golden Retrievers are very fond of carrying things, such as sticks or toys, in their mouths wherever they go

  • They have very soft mouths to the point where they can hold a raw egg in their mouths without cracking it
